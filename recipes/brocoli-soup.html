Broccoli Cheese Soup

This broccoli cheese soup is very flavorful.

It's great for serving at luncheons or special gatherings with a quiche.

To make it a little fancier, add 1 cup sliced mushrooms and 1 cup white wine with the onions.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli
  • 4 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (1 pund) loaf processed cheese food, cubed
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • ⅔ cup cornstarch
  • 1 cup water

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Melt butter in a stockpot over medium heat. Add onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened.

    Stir broccoli. Add broth and simmer until broccoli is tender, 10 to 15 minutes.

  2. Step 2

    3. Reduce heat; add cheese cubes and stir until melted. Stir in milk and garlic powder.

  3. Step 3

    4. Stir cornstarch and water together in a small bowl until smooth.

    Stir into soup; cook, stirring frequently, until thick.

  4. Step 4

    5. Enjoy your meal.
